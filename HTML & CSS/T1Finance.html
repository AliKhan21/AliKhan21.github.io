Personal Finance


This course provides a survey of the major economic decisions facing the typical American household and examines the influence of social and economic changeon individual financial planning. Students acquire the knowledge and develop the necessary analytical skills to make informed choices related to topics such as managing finances and budgeting, banking and saving, earning and reporting income, buying goods and services, using credit, and protecting against risk. This course puts emphasis on goal setting, life long learning, and active decision making.

  • I learned how to manange my finances.
  • I learned how to calculate taxes.
  • I learned the differece between saving money and investing it.
  • I learned the benefit and cons of a credit card.
  • I learned how important it is to report all your income.
