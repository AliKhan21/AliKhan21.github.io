PC Hardware & Diagnostics


This course teaches computer hardware concepts related to installation, configuration, and upgrading of motherboards, processors, and memory. Additional topics covered include diagnosing, troubleshooting, and preventive maintenance.

  • I learned about the different kinds of ram and they work.
  • I learned how to upgrade a PC's internals.
  • I learned how to use the BIOS screen, enable and disable slots.
  • I learned how to toubleshoot a PC.
  • I learned how to build a computer.
