Business Comunications II


This course continues the development of the students ability to produce clear and effective business communication. Assignments support the writing and speaking demands that students will face in a business environment and will focus on proper utilization of word choice, mechanics, and style.

  • I learned how to write complete and complex sentences.
  • I learned how to breakdown a complex sentence.
  • I learned how to use proper grammar.
  • I learned how to use gerunds in a proper sentence.
  • I learned how to take effective notes and study.
